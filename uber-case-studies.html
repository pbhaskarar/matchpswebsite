/Product Engineering and Design

Uber

A team of six engineers and UX designers from Matchpoint had OS-agnostic apps conceptualized, built, and deployed in Uber's App Ecosystem in less than 2 months, rapidly empowering them with new revenue opportunities in the pandemic.

/Challenge

The Challenge

Uber Concepts and Studio teams were looking to quarterly deploy a new App/Service

within their App Ecosystem to help them find alternative revenue opportunities in select markets in the wake of COVID-19.

Loss of revenue due to COVID-19

Lack of a large in-house team of expert engineers

Lack of new and alternative revenue sources

Difficult to scale the team fast enough to timely solve the problem

/Solution

The Solution

Matchpoint provided Uber with an expert team of six engineers and UX designers to deliver platform-agnostic apps with the first roll-out in less than 2 months.

Short turnaround time

Delivery across Native iOS, Android, Web

End-to-end support from early conceptualization to actual deployment

Good UX to speak to the target audience in the select markets for driving revenue

Results

In a matter of a few months, Uber was able to re-envision the mobile experience
for their users, quickly roll out new products, and share information here on the
revenue gained or the new opportunities discovered by the customer, if we can
get that data> with the help of Matchpoint Solutions.
